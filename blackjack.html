Blackjack

This game is very simple. Play against the dealer as cards are dealt. Press hit to get a new card and hold when you are done. Try to get at close to 21 without going over as possible.
This game was designed to show my students how cards can be objects and a deck is a special object that has an array that holds the car objects. The card and deck objects were designed so that they could be used in different games. This is one such game that could be made with them.
