Article Site

The article web app was designed to allow a collection of article writers the ability to publish articles using a rich text editor. The admin can create different categories and make posts featured on the front page. In addition to featured articles the front page displays previews of the latest 9 articles written. This web app could easily be used for news sites, hobby sites, or blog sites. The Git respository for this web app can be found at https://github.com/PhillipAC/article_site
