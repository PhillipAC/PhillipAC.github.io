Connect Colors Games 2-Player

Compete against a friend to get 6 squares of your color in a row or diagonal. You take turns dropping squares. When you are done and want to play again just right click. If you wish to move the board around you can use the arrow keys.
This game was made to show of grid based gameplay. In addition the gameboard can be any size with any number of rows and columns. This creates a very customizable gameboard by just adjusted a few numbers in declearing the board. You can even have more than one board running at a time.
