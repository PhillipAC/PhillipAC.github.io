Bridges & Shortcuts

This will be a turned based board game that players 1-4 can play. Once again this project shows the power of Object-Oriented programming.

Rules

Objective: Be the first player to reach the end of the path though the woods.
Spaces: There are 3 types of spaces in the game. Spaces on the path end a turn. Spaces on a bridge will wash you back to either the first space or just past the last bridge you crossed. Spaces in front of a shortcut will let you take it skipping large parts of the path!
Turns: When it is your turn roll the dice and the game will move you that many spaces. You do not have to roll exact spaces to win the game.
