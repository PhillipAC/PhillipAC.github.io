Wrestling Mania

This is a purly fun game I made to bring to a wrestling party. I set the game up with a Makey Makey to make it so my friends have to use their full bodies to compete.
Gameplay is simple. Player 1 uses WASD and Player 2 uses the arrow keys.

  • LEFT/RIGHT: LEFT/RIGHT or A/D
  • Reach: UP or W
  • Duck: DOWN or S
The objective of the game is to exhaust the other player. Reaching for someone will exhaust you but if you can grab the other player press the reach button over and over to cause them exhaustion. If you are grabbed you will need to press reach over and over to break free. Every so often a chair might appear. Walk over to it to throw the chair. If a chair is coming for you you can press duck to escape. You can also duck to rest, but while ducking you cannot reach for the other player leaving you open to being grabbed.
