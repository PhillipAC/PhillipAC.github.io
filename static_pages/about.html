About

My name is Phillip Clontz and I am a math teacher at New Hope high School. I currently teach a range of courses including Geometry, Algebra 2, Pre-Calculus, and AP Calculus.

In addition to core classes I teach programming as an elective course. In this class I use Khan Academy's programming course to structure the class. This teaches students how to program from nothing using processingJS to interact with the HTML5 Canvas.

Outside of classes I also coach the New Hope Scholars Bowl Team. Information about Scholars Bowl can be found at Madison County's Scholars Bowl Website.
